# STAR WARS: ANOTHER ONE
## A New Key to Success

**Written by: The Galaxy's Greatest DJ**

---

## ACT I: THE DESERT OF TATOOINE

*Scene opens on the desert planet of Tatooine. DJ KHALED (as Luke Skywalker) stands outside his moisture farm, wearing traditional Jedi robes with gold chains visible underneath.*

**DJ KHALED**: Another one! Another moisture vaporator fixed! Bless up!

*UNCLE OWEN approaches*

**UNCLE OWEN**: Luke, stop yelling "another one" every time you fix something.

**DJ KHALED**: Uncle Owen, you don't understand. Every vaporator is a KEY TO SUCCESS. They don't want us to have clean water!

*R2-D2 rolls up, beeping frantically*

**DJ KHALED**: Yo! A droid! We the best droids! What's your name, little homie?

*R2-D2 beeps*

**DJ KHALED**: R2-D2? I'ma call you... ANOTHER ONE!

## ACT II: MEETING OBI-WAN

*DJ KHALED and R2-D2 venture into the Jundland Wastes. They are attacked by Tusken Raiders.*

**DJ KHALED**: Oh no! They don't want us to find Obi-Wan! MAJOR KEY ALERT!

*OBI-WAN KENOBI appears, scaring away the raiders*

**OBI-WAN**: Hello there. I'm Obi-Wan Kenobi.

**DJ KHALED**: BEN! The legendary Jedi! Listen, I got a hologram here from Princess Leia. She says "Help me, Obi-Wan Kenobi, you're my only hope."

**OBI-WAN**: Your father wanted you to have this when you were old enough. *hands over lightsaber*

**DJ KHALED**: *ignites blue lightsaber* MAJOR KEY! This is the KEY TO THE FORCE! WE THE BEST JEDI!

**OBI-WAN**: You must come with me to Alderaan and learn the ways of the Force.

**DJ KHALED**: Say no more! I'm on my way to greatness! Another one! Let's go save the princess! LION!

## ACT IV: THE DEATH STAR

*The Millennium Falcon is captured by the Death Star*

**DJ KHALED**: They don't want us to escape! But we gonna overcome!

**OBI-WAN**: I must go disable the tractor beam.

**DJ KHALED**: Major key! While you do that, I'ma go rescue the princess! Another one!

*DJ Khaled finds Princess Leia's cell*

**DJ KHALED**: Princess Leia! It's me, DJ Khaled Skywalker! I'm here to rescue you!

**PRINCESS LEIA**: You're who?

**DJ KHALED**: We the best rescue team! Come on, we gotta go! MAJOR KEY ALERT!

**LEIA**: Aren't you a little loud for a Stormtrooper?

**DJ KHALED**: They don't want me to be quiet! Let's go!

*They run through corridors*

**DJ KHALED**: Another one! *blasts Stormtrooper* Another one! *blasts another* ANOTHER ONE!

**LEIA**: Can you stop yelling that?

**DJ KHALED**: Never! It's the key to success!

**DJ KHALED**: Wait! I got an idea! *pulls out phone* I'ma Snapchat this! The pathway to success is social media presence! We gotta document everything!

**DJ KHALED**: Always! *snaps selfie* Bless up! Suffering from success in a trash compactor!

**DJ KHALED**: R2! ANOTHER ONE! You the real MVP! MAJOR KEY!

## ACT VI: THE FINAL BATTLE

*The Rebel Alliance prepares to attack the Death Star*

**REBEL COMMANDER**: The Death Star has one weakness - this exhaust port.

**DJ KHALED**: Say no more! I got the keys! The KEYS TO SUCCESS! I'm going in!

*DJ Khaled flies an X-Wing toward the Death Star*

**DJ KHALED**: *over comms* Red Leader standing by!

**PILOT**: That's... that's not your call sign.

**DJ KHALED**: WE THE BEST PILOTS! Let's go!

*TIE Fighters attack*

**DJ KHALED**: They don't want us to destroy the Death Star! Another one down! *destroys TIE Fighter* ANOTHER ONE! *destroys another*

*DJ Khaled approaches the exhaust port*

**OBI-WAN'S VOICE**: Use the Force, Luke.

**DJ KHALED**: Ben! Is that you? Bless up! I'm about to use the Force! This is a MAJOR KEY!

**OBI-WAN'S VOICE**: Trust your feelings.

**DJ KHALED**: I trust the keys! The keys to success! The Force is the ultimate key!

*DJ Khaled closes his eyes and uses the Force*

**DJ KHALED**: ANOTHER ONE! *fires proton torpedoes*

*The Death Star explodes*

**DJ KHALED**: WE THE BEST REBELS! MAJOR KEY VICTORY! They didn't want us to win, but we did! LION ORDER! Bless up to the whole galaxy!
